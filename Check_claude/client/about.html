About Bella Cucina

Discover the story, passion, and people behind our authentic Italian cuisine

Our Story

Bella Cucina was born from a deep love of Italian cuisine and the desire to bring authentic flavors of Italy to Foodville. Founded in 2010 by the Romano family, our restaurant has been serving traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Chef Marco Romano, who trained in Naples and worked in some of Italy's finest restaurants, returned to America with a dream to create a dining experience that would transport guests straight to Italy. Using his grandmother's cherished recipes and techniques, he established Bella Cucina as a place where quality ingredients and traditional methods come together to create extraordinary meals.

What started as a small family-owned trattoria has grown into a beloved local institution, all while maintaining the warmth, passion, and commitment to authentic Italian cuisine that has defined us from the beginning. Each dish tells a story of tradition, family, and the rich culinary heritage of Italy.

Our Values

Authenticity

We stay true to traditional Italian recipes and cooking methods, never compromising on quality or taking shortcuts that would diminish the authentic experience.

Quality Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients, from imported Italian specialties to fresh local produce, ensuring every dish meets our high standards of excellence.

Family Spirit

We treat our staff and customers like family, creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home and part of our story.
